Venue

RC 2023 will take place in Giessen, Germany, a lively town with two universities and about 35.000 students.
Giessen is easily reachable via train (approx. 45 Minutes from Frankfurt central station), via plane to Frankfurt International Airport (approx. 1h 15 to Giessen via train), or by car (Autobahn A5 and A45).
